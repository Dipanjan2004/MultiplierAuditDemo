FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Q1 Why can't our SEO agency just handle this?
It's not a gap in their work; it's a gap in their expertise. There's no SEO platform that monitors ChatGPT and Perplexity citations. No analytics suite that flags AI-sourced visitors. No dashboard that shows AI-attributed revenue. We built those tools. That's the difference.
Q2 We already have an SEO agency, and we're happy with them.
Keep them. We sit alongside, not in place of. Our code ships into the repo they already work in, with comments and tests so they can own ongoing optimization.
Q3 Is AI search actually big enough to matter?
It's not the majority of queries yet — it's the majority of high-intent queries. Buyers researching a multi-thousand-dollar decision are disproportionately on AI first. The revenue concentration is already there; the analytics just haven't caught up.
Q4 How do you actually prove revenue came from AI?
A combination of referrer signals, first-touch URL fingerprints, and a synthetic query-probe system we run against the major models. We match inbound traffic to the exact answer that sent them, then follow the session through to closed revenue in your CRM.
Q5 What results should I expect?
By day 30, you'll see yourself appearing in AI answers for the queries in your category. By day 60–90, attributed pipeline. We don't promise numbers because categories differ — we promise the infrastructure that makes the numbers legible.
